Here are some photos of the destinations :

Bangalore

Bengaluru also called as Bangalore is the capital of the state Karnataka. Bangalore is the place for emerging start-ups and innovation centers. The city is also known for its night-life and places like Cubbon park, Vidhana Soudha etc.

Chennai

Chennai is located on the Bay of Bengal in eastern india, the capital of state Tamil Nadu. Chennai is a beautiful home to some of the Historic buildings like Fort. Saint goerge which was built in 1644 , British military garrison and East india company.

Hyderabad

Hyderabad is the captial of the state Telengana. It is a major city for tech industry. The food item "Biryani" is very popular in this state. You shouldn't miss the biryani whenever you come to Hyderabad! Historic sites like Golconda fort and famous diamond centers are located in this place.

New delhi

New delhi is the capital of India. New delhi is the home and place for all the three important places like Government of India, Rashtrapati Bhavanand the parliament House.

Mumbai

Mumbai was formerly called as Bombay. It is one of the highly and densely populated city in India. Gateway of India and stone arch are some of the places located in this city. There are many caves and temples located in this place apart from the historic places. The city is also famous and heart of the Bollywood fim industry.
